The complete solution

We bring our expertise to your service to build, expand, or renovate a customized dealership tailored to your needs, achieving results that meet your ambitions.

  • We understand your reality

    Drawing on our experience in the automotive industry, we understand the specific challenges of each dealership.

  • We optimize

    We dedicate time to thoroughly studying your needs and optimizing every detail of your future space.

  • We go further

    We are available even after delivery. Are your needs evolving after a few years? We will find solutions that adapt to these changes.

The dealership construction
process simplified

  • 01.

    Pre-Construction

    Although often underestimated, the preliminary phase before construction is the foundation on which the entire project rests. Our team supports you from A to Z, from selecting the site to building your dealership.

    • Site research and acquisition

      We help you identify the most suitable site for your project, considering factors such as visibility, accessibility, the surrounding environment, and municipal regulations.

    • Discussions with municipalities and soil assessment

      We handle all administrative procedures with municipalities and other public bodies while ensuring compliance with environmental standards.

    • Plan development and budgeting

      We create a detailed plan in close collaboration with you and the manufacturer to design a functional and aesthetic space that aligns with the brand's image guidelines. We take the time to analyze project costs and establish an accurate budget.

    • Manufacturer partnerships

      With over 35 years of experience in the automotive industry, we have built a strong relationship of trust with all manufacturers.

  • 02.

    Construction

    We provide a turnkey solution, from design to delivery, including obtaining permits and coordinating all trades. Our expertise even extends to assisting you in selecting your equipment, furnishings, and initial inventory, all while adhering to the builder's standards.

    • Permit acquisition

      We handle all construction permit applications.

    • Subcontractor oversight

      Our experienced team is on-site to coordinate the various trades (design, subcontracting, suppliers) and ensure the smooth progression of each project phase.

    • Collaboration with suppliers

      Whether it’s for your exterior and interior signage, office furniture, or garage equipment, we handle all the details of your setup with the various suppliers.

    • Meetings with the manufacturer

      We are always there to support you during meetings with the manufacturer to effectively protect your interests.

  • 03.

    Post-Construction

    Our experts are here to assist you in all phases of your project, including post-construction. Whether it involves adjustments, modifications, or finishing touches, we tailor our services to your specific needs. The automotive world is constantly evolving, and so is your dealership. We are by your side to guide you through the various changes to your building.

    • Continuous improvement

      We understand that the needs and realities of your dealership may change. That’s why we support you in the evolution and adaptation of your building (expansion, renovation, electrification, technologies, etc.).
